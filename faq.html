Renting a Car Under the Age of 25

How old do I have to be to rent a car with Keva Auto Rentals LLC?

The minimum age to rent a vehicle with Keva Auto Rentals LLC is 20 years old throughout the U.S. and Canada, and 18 years old in New York and Michigan.

Can I rent a car at age 18?

Yes. Renting a car at age 18 is possible in a few specific instances. See below.

• U.S. Government Exceptions: If someone is a United States military personnel or Government Employee and can provide official government paperwork to the rental location in which they are inquiring, they will be able to rent a car at age 18. 

What is a Young Renter Fee?

A Young Renter Fee (also called an Age Differential Charge or Underage Driver’s Fee) is an additional fee that is applied along with the standard car rental rate to reservations of drivers ages 20-24.

What cars can I rent when under 25?

Keva Auto Rentals LLC car renters who are ages 20 years old and up can rent most classes of vehicles, except certain specialty car classes.

Why is there an age limit to rent cars?

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration reports that drivers under the age of 25 are statistically more likely to be involved in auto accidents than drivers who are over the age of 25. Statistics of auto accidents involving drivers ages 25 and up are dramatically lower than the accident rates of young drivers.

 

However, safe and qualifying drivers who are between the ages of 20 and 24 (or ages 18-24 in Michigan and New York) are able to rent cars with Keva Auto Rentals LLC. In order to do so, young renters will be subject to a Young Renter Fee.

The minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21 years of age. There is no maximum restriction in the United States and Canada. See Exceptions below.

• A 'Young Renter Fee' may be applied to your rental, if you are between the ages of 21 and 24.

• The average cost is approximately $20.00 per day but can be higher or lower

Rental Insurance

Is a rental car covered by my insurance?

Generally, coverage from your primary auto insurance will extend to a rental vehicle. If you cause an accident while driving the rental, your liability insurance would pay up to your policy limits for the damages to other cars or property.

Is it worth it to get insurance on rental car?

The collision damage waiver, also known as optional vehicle protection or loss damage waiver, can cost as much as $19 per day and shifts liability for collision damage from the person renting the car to the company. However, your personal auto insurance should already include liability insurance

Do credit cards cover insurance on rental cars?

Not every card affords the same coverage. Most Visa cards will cover theft, damage to the rental car, towing and loss-of-use charges if you do not have a personal auto insurance policy. However, both Visa and Mastercard exclude injury, property damage and damage to other vehicles, according to website.

Do insurance companies pay for rental cars?

If your car is totaled, many companies will pay for your rental as a courtesy, but they are not required to do so. If you are filing a claim with your own insurance company, the cost of a rental car will only be covered if you paid a premium to include rentalreimbursement coverage in your policy.
